Ingredients

  • Paneer
  • Paneer Masala
  • Tomato
  • onion
  • oil
  • ginger-garlic paste
  • jira
  • water
  • salt
  • turmeric

Steps needed to prepare paneer Masala

  1. Take some oil in a pan.
  2. After heating oil, add jira and ginger-garlic paste in he oil.
  3. Then add tomato and onion purie in it.
  4. cook it on slow flame for some time.
  5. After that add paneer masala, tumeric and salt as per taste.
  6. Then add some water and cook for some time .
  7. Add fried paneer pieces in the purie.
  8. Add some corriender and serve it.
  9. Enjoy your dish.
